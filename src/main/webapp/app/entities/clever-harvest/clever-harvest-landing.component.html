Olivarera Los Pedroches SCA

Córdoba, Spain

Olivarera los pedroches is a cooperative founded in 1957 for the olive oil production in the North of Cordoba Spain. After 1995 they have turned all their production into organic. And now they are producing as well biodynamic olive oil. They are more than just a cooperative. Its partners combine their effort and commitment to the maintenance of a mountain olive grove and the social and economic development of the region of the Valle de Los Pedroches

Picual

 
▼   Picual

Picual covers an area of more than 1,000,000 ha. in Spain. It is grown mainly in the province of Jaén, although it also has an important presence in Córdoba, Granada and Seville. Its cultivation is increasing in Spain and in the world because it is a variety of olive tree with high productive yield. Picual olives have a high fat yield, which allows for greater profitability than other varieties. The intensive olive groves of much of Spain are dedicated to the planting of this kind of olive tree. Picual olive oil has high oxidation stability and has high amounts of Oleocanthal.
